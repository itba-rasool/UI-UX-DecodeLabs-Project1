# Fitness App UX Research

## Project Overview
This project focuses on UX research for a Fitness App designed for busy office professionals who want to stay fit but struggle to find time for regular workouts.

The project includes:
- User Persona
- Empathy Map
- User Goals
- Pain Points Analysis
- User Needs & Behaviors

## User Persona

### Ahmed Khan
- Age: 29
- Occupation: Software Engineer
- Location: Karachi, Pakistan

### Goals
- Stay physically fit and healthy
- Reduce work-related stress
- Improve daily energy levels
- Follow a workout routine despite a busy schedule

### Pain Points
- Lack of time for gym visits
- Long working hours
- Difficulty maintaining consistency
- Expensive gym memberships
- Low motivation

## Empathy Map

The empathy map analyzes the user's:

### Thinks
- I should exercise regularly
- I don't have enough time for the gym
- I need a flexible workout plan

### Feels
- Stressed due to work
- Guilty for skipping workouts
- Frustrated by lack of consistency

### Says
- I want to get fit
- I need quick workout options
- Gym memberships are expensive

### Does
- Uses smartphone frequently
- Tracks fitness activities
- Searches for quick home workouts

## User Needs
- 10–20 minute workout plans
- Personalized fitness recommendations
- Progress tracking
- Daily reminders and motivation

## Expected Benefits
- Improved health
- Reduced stress
- Better productivity
- Increased energy levels
- Flexible fitness routines
